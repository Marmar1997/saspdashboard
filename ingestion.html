I. What ingestion is and is not in SASP/01

Ingestion in SASP covers layers 1–3 of an 8-layer architecture[verify]: Data Sources → Source Ranking / Channel Analysis → Raw-to-Social-Signal Extraction. The differentiator lives downstream in layers 4–6 — epistemic structuring into Knowledge Objects, archetype generation as KO activation patterns, and the simulation itself. Ingestion is the foundation that makes those downstream layers worth running: a clean, traceable, country-deep substrate of language, belief, and distribution. If ingestion is opaque or unrepresentative, every downstream layer inherits the problem.

What ingestion is NOT
  • Not "more data = better." Volume past the representativeness frontier is noise, and noise compounds through the KO layer. A 500-signal corpus that mirrors Finanzaonline's user skew is worse than a 100-signal corpus that doesn't.
  • Not Reddit-first. Reddit under-represents Italian mortgage language by an order of magnitude versus Finanzaonline and the Italian intermediary editorial ecosystem. Defaulting to Reddit is a tell that the ingestion is not country-deep.
  • Not scrape-everything. The legal posture is public-only, paraphrased, no authenticated scraping. TOS compliance is a per-source design decision, not a default.

The frame to hold: representativeness, not volume.

II. The methodological line/02

Two source families, two roles, never mixed in the same weighting step.

Qualitative → Knowledge Objects

Forums & first-person sources feed belief.

Finanzaonline threads, intermediary reader-comment surfaces, paraphrased forum exchanges — these are the qualitative substrate for belief, fear, heuristic, idiom, distrust-pattern.

They tell us what the audience actually says, what language they recognize, what shame layers exist (e.g. garante familiare as both practical recourse and identity marker, KO-10).

Quantitative → Distribution shape

Official sources feed weight.

ISTAT, MEF, Banca d'Italia, Consap, Agenzia delle Entrate, BCE / ECB — the quantitative substrate for population weighting, age distribution, regional intensity, regime forfettario vs. ordinario splits, ISEE cliff dynamics, rate-environment context.

These feed the audience-generation layer (5), not the KO layer (4).

— The failure mode — Mixing the two. Counting Finanzaonline post-volume as a proxy for population frequency, or treating ISTAT averages as belief signals, collapses two distinct epistemic inputs into one noisy aggregate. Forum scrape volume does not estimate Italy. The two families meet only at layer 5, under explicit, documented weighting rules.

IV. Pipeline architecture — layers 1–3/04
Layer 1Data Sources
Per-source-type connectors: HTML/RSS crawler for public web (forum threads, intermediary editorial, press), PDF parser for bank trasparenza documents, structured-data fetchers for official statistics endpoints (ISTAT, MEF, Agenzia delle Entrate Osservatorio). One connector class per source family, each with its own rate limits, TOS, and authentication posture documented. For v0.1 the connectors are stand-ins for hand extraction; the abstraction holds and the production swap is plumbing.
Layer 2Source Ranking & Channel Analysis
Per-item relevance scoring against the active corpus theme set (A through F for the mortgage case), freshness weighting (recency-decayed for time-sensitive themes like rate environment, neutral for durable heuristics like garante shame), platform-bias annotation (Finanzaonline-heavy items flagged as urban/male-skewed per the corpus disclosure), and near-duplicate clustering at the paraphrase level so the same heuristic repeated across five intermediary articles registers as one signal, not five.
Layer 3Raw → Social Signal
Each raw item passes through a paraphraser that preserves semantic content while breaking verbatim string match (copyright posture); a source-tagger that records URL, date, platform, and access path; a theme assigner that maps to the active corpus taxonomy; and a native-Italian human-in-the-loop validator who catches what the paraphraser misses — irony, regional idiom, affective register, the difference between atipico as legal category and atipico as identity wound.
V. Validation and human-in-the-loop/05

Native-Italian validation step

A validator with Italian as a working language reviews each signal — not as a translation gate, but as an interpretive gate. They detect irony ("fisso, almeno sai come muori" — humorous and serious at once), catch regional idiom and tax-regime-specific vocabulary, distinguish atipico as legal category from atipico as identity wound, and flag platform-bias drift (e.g. a theme drifting Finanzaonline-heavy and needing counter-balancing). v0.1 is ad-hoc and single-validator; production is multi-validator with inter-rater agreement.

Conflict-resolution protocol

When sources disagree on a substantive claim — bank product page says a polizza is "obbligatoria", forum heuristic says only incendio is legally mandatory — the default rule is to preserve both as separate KOs with their relations annotated (contradicts, see KO-05 ↔ bank framing of ProteggiMutuo). Resolution-by-truth is out of scope at the ingestion layer. The contradiction itself is the signal, and surfacing it is what SASP delivers downstream.

Freshness checks & signal expiry

Time-sensitive signals (rate-environment narratives, regulatory window dates like the Consap renewal through 31 Dec 2027) carry an expiry; durable heuristics (atipico identity, garante shame, TAEG vs TAN literacy) do not. Expired signals are retired, not deleted — retained for longitudinal comparison and audit.

Why automated-only is insufficient

Paraphrase quality, irony detection, affective register, platform-bias correction, and country-specific tax/regulatory nuance are all places where automation systematically loses precisely what makes the SASP corpus differentiated from a generic LLM persona. A generic ingestion pipeline produces a generic output. The HITL step is the structural feature that prevents drift to the mean.

VII. Legal & GDPR posture/07
  • Public sources only, no authenticated scraping. No login walls, no OAuth-gated APIs, no paywalled content. Each connector is reviewed against source TOS before going live.
  • All content paraphrased, never reproduced verbatim. The paraphraser preserves semantic content while breaking exact string match. This is the corpus-side disclosure already in files/01_signal_corpus.md and the posture we hold publicly.
  • No PII collected or stored. Forum-level handles, where retained, are at the granularity already public on the source platform and are not linked to identity. No emails, names, addresses, phone numbers, or fiscal codes.
  • Production client deployments include DPIA setup. A Data Protection Impact Assessment is part of every client-facing production deployment, per the privacy Q&A in files/07_demo_narrative.md.
  • TOS compliance per source. Reddit API restrictions since 2023[verify] make Reddit-based ingestion increasingly costly and bandwidth-capped — not material for the Italian mortgage use case, relevant for any future non-IT scope. Facebook groups are closed unless joined manually[verify] and are explicitly out of scope for automated ingestion.
VIII. What this is not — the warnings/08
  • — 01 —NOT a substitute for live customer research with the bank's actual customers. SASP simulates a synthetic audience grounded in public Italian signals. It complements — does not replace — direct customer interviews, branch-level qualitative work, or transactional cohort analysis that only the bank's own data can support.
  • — 02 —NOT a global broad-and-shallow ingestion. SASP is EU-native and country-deep by design. Buyers wanting a one-pipeline-thirty-countries platform should go elsewhere — that is a different product with different trade-offs, and we should not pretend to be it.
  • — 03 —NOT a justification for compensating thin local data with cross-country volume. Re-using French forum signals to fill gaps in Italian coverage breaks the country-layer logic the architecture exists to protect. Every country layer stands on its own corpus, validators, and distributional anchors — or it does not stand.

Methodology · v0.1 demo

Ingestion in v0.1 is curated, paraphrased, source-tagged, theme-assigned, and hand-validated. Sources drawn exclusively from the corpus references in files/01_signal_corpus.md and files/03_distribution_data.md. No external sources invented; no claims unsupported by the pack added without an explicit [verify] marker for spot-checking before Sunday.

Production scales the same methodology with automated connectors (~60% target[verify]) layered under a multi-validator HITL step (~40%). Country-deep, not broad-and-shallow.
