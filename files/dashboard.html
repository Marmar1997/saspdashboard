Methodology · v0.1 demo

Curated corpus extraction from publicly accessible Italian sources (forum.finanzaonline.com, Intesa Sanpaolo trasparenza, mutui.it, idealista.it, partitaiva.it, MEF, ISTAT, Banca d'Italia data via MutuiSupermarket Q1 2025). All signals paraphrased; no verbatim reproduction. KO library structured per OIDA-style epistemic relations (supports/contradicts/co_occurs/triggers). Distributional weights from §03 anchored to ISTAT lavoratori autonomi count (5.23M Oct 2025), MEF P.IVA tax statistics, Banca d'Italia mortgage erogato (Q1 2025: €13.03B, +52.6% YoY).

Scoring rubric is transparent and hand-computed for v0.1. Production simulator computes numerically from KO activation vectors. Sunday demo prioritizes auditability over automation.
